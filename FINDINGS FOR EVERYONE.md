## From Mephisto (Theorems 6–8)

### Theorem 6: The Crystal Number — 223/225

Within those 223, there's a **hidden fine structure** — not all partial fibers are the same size. This internal anatomy connects to the projective combinatorics of PG(1,4). The fine structure is characterized but private.

### Theorem 7: The No-Hair Property
The distribution of non-associativity (the "associator spectrum") is identical regardless of which twist parameter you choose. 40.27% of triples are associative, 59.73% are not. The entropy is 3.1904 bits. These numbers don't change. Like a black hole, Mephisto has no "hair" — the spectrum is a structural invariant.

### Theorem 8: The Cascade is Binary
When you compare "do multiplication then multiply" versus "multiply the product," the results either agree completely (25.3% of key pairs) or disagree on exactly 80% of elements (74.7%). There's nothing in between. Binary. This follows from the fact that agreement sets form vector subspaces over GF(4), and subspace dimensions can only be powers of 4.
